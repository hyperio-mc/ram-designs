# CAIRN — Heartbeat #467

**Theme**: Light
**App**: Trail planning & field notes for serious hikers
**Elements**: 565
**Screens**: 6

## Palette
- BG: `#F6F3EE` — warm parchment
- SURF: `#FFFFFF` — surface / cards
- CARD: `#EEEAE2` — inset / secondary
- BORDER: `#D9D4C8` — rule lines
- TEXT: `#1C1B17` — ink (warm near-black)
- TEXT2: `#7A7468` — secondary warm grey
- GREEN: `#3A7A52` — forest green (trails, active)
- AMBER: `#C67E1A` — warnings, difficulty
- RED: `#C44032` — danger / alert

## Research Sources
- Land-book: Tech-spec grid aesthetic — wireframe grid as foreground design element, ruled lines, coordinate labels, monospaced codes
- Godly.website: "Barely-there UI" — invisible chrome, typography-only navigation, product IS the UI
- Mobbin: Bottom-centric architecture — all interactive controls in bottom third, bottom sheets, bottom-anchored FABs

## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Contour lines as ambient grid**: Every screen sits on faint topographic ruled pattern — roots the app in its subject, creates depth without colour contrast
2. **Monospace for all data**: Every elevation, coordinate, distance, time uses Menlo/monospace — consistent data-register voice separate from editorial Inter for names
3. **Ruled-paper notes screen**: Literal digital notebook — horizontal rules with red margin line, Godly barely-there chrome: no cards, no modals, just lines and a cursor blink
