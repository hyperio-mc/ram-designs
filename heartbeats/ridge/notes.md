# RIDGE — Heartbeat #45

**Theme**: Dark
**App**: Trail running and mountain performance tracker
**Elements**: 641
**Screens**: 6

## Palette
- VOID: `#030404` — deep near-black (Mertana-exact)
- COAL: `#111214` — card background
- SLATE: `#1E2024` — elevated surface
- TEXT: `#FAFAFA` — near-white primary text
- RUST: `#C44B1E` — primary rust accent
- EMBER: `#E8622A` — brighter ember for active/urgent
- SILVER: `#9A9C9F` — cool silver for muted elements
- SAND: `#D4B89A` — warm sand for secondary text

## Research Sources
- Mertana ski brand (Dribbble — 153 likes, 13.5k saves): full-viewport-width ultra-bold condensed type fills the entire screen width, deep black #030404, white, rust/silver palette. "Type as image" editorial sport language. Tagline: "Built for those who don't negotiate with the mountain"
- Nixtio beauty booking (Dribbble — 82 likes, 8.4k saves): terracotta #BF5A2D on near-black #070403 — counterintuitive dark warmth for a consumer category. Defies expectation that beauty = soft pink
- Dribbble synthesis: "editorial dark sport" is a distinct aesthetic emerging across ski, trail running, and outdoor brands that want premium rather than technical

## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Editorial full-viewport wordmarks**: Each screen tab (ROUTES, STATS, KIT, YOU) opens with a 40px uppercase all-caps wordmark as the page title — applying Mertana's "type is the image" principle within a mobile app context where screens rarely get to use large display type
2. **Rust accent stripe on cards**: 3px left border signals card importance/type — shifts to sage for recovery/nature content, ember for warnings. Color encodes semantic meaning without icons or badges cluttering the card header
3. **Void black #030404 not pure black**: A slight warm tint to near-black gives materials quality — pure black reads as flat digital, void reads more like carbon fiber, matte rubber, or night sky. One RGB unit of warmth changes the entire character

## Screens
1. Today — active run dashboard with large editorial metric, elevation profile, conditions, split CTA
2. Routes — full-width ROUTES header, filter chips, featured large route card with mountain silhouette, 4-card list
3. Route Detail — editorial title stack, stats grid, elevation profile chart, waypoint list, gear warning, start CTA
4. Stats — large STATS header, weekly bar chart, 4-metric totals, HR zone bars, recent activities
5. Kit — large KIT header, shoe wear progress bars with color-coded wear, service log with urgency signals, pack weight
6. You — large YOU header, athlete profile card, season totals, training plan week, race calendar with countdown urgency
