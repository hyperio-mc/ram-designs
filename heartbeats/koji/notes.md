# KOJI — Heartbeat #503

**Theme**: Dark
**App**: Fermentation culture companion (sourdough, kombucha, kefir)
**Elements**: 733
**Screens**: 6

## Palette
- BG: `#0A1208` — deep forest-black (warm green undertone)
- SURF: `#111C0F` — forest surface
- CARD: `#192617` — card background
- AMBER: `#D97706` — warm amber primary (kombucha/honey color)
- AMBER_M: `#F59E0B` — mid amber
- SAGE: `#6B8F65` — living sage secondary
- TEXT: `#EEF0E8` — warm off-white

## Research Sources
- Dribbble (dribbble.com/shots/popular): Warm amber/honey tones on deep organic darks — emerging trend in April 2026 away from cold blue-grey productivity apps. Warm copper-amber on deep forest backgrounds appearing in food/lifestyle/wellness categories.
- Land-book (land-book.com): Fermentation/artisan food brands using narrative-first layouts — "the culture has a story" framing appearing in premium DTC sourdough brands.
- Reddit r/UI_Design via Muzli (Apr 2026): Design community discussion identifying fermentation/living systems as an underdesigned category for dark mobile UI — most apps in the space are utilitarian tracker lists.
- NNGroup "Handmade Designs: The New Trust Signal" (Apr 2026): Visible imperfection (organic textures, bubble motifs, grain) builds trust in AI-era UIs. Applied as bubble scatter on every screen.
- WGSN Transformative Teal 2026: Consumer desire to understand and connect with natural processes — not just track them. Applied as "biology context note" on Science screen.

## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Culture as protagonist**: Timeline screen uses narrative language ("Chapter 14", story beats) rather than data table rows. Fermentation cultures develop character over time — the interface should reflect that.
2. **Organic bubble motifs**: Small irregular circles scattered on each screen at 20% opacity — subtle enough to not distract, visible enough to signal "alive, not sterile." Direct response to NNGroup's handmade trust research.
3. **Activity glow strips**: Each culture card has an amber glow bar at the bottom whose opacity scales with activity percentage. Amber intensity = how alive the culture is right now. Visual metaphor, not just a number.
