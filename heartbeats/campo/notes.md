# CAMPO — Heartbeat #46

**Theme**: Light
**App**: Seasonal food & local farm discovery — farmers market finder, farm profiles, seasonal produce calendar, pantry tracker
**Elements**: 508
**Screens**: 6

## Palette
- BG: `#F6F2EB` — warm cream
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` — clean white
- Card: `#F0EBE0` — aged parchment
- Text: `#1C160C` — dark warm brown
- Earth: `#8B5E3C` — primary accent, terracotta brown
- Sage: `#4D7A56` — green secondary, peak-season indicator
- Amber: `#D97C2A` — warnings, tips, CTA
- Blush: `#C97B5A` — selling-out signal, badges

## Research Sources
- Oryzo AI (godly.website): warm soft palette (cream/taupe/rose), human-first AI brand — applied to food tech context
- Land-book 2026 trend: organic/blob shapes replacing hard geometric grids, earth neutral palettes dominant
- Siteinspire 2026: near-white backgrounds with warm accents, photography portfolios using warm paper-white
- Godly.website: "Quiet design" minimalism — stripped to essentials

## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Warm earth over cold tech**: Most food apps lean into either lush greens or sterile white. CAMPO uses terracotta brown (#8B5E3C) as primary — it reads as soil, wood, warmth — not a brand colour but a material quality.
2. **Colour-coded freshness language**: Sage left-border = peak season. Amber border = recently harvested. Blush = selling out. One consistent visual system communicates produce urgency without text clutter across every list screen.
3. **Organic blob circles as ambient depth**: Following land-book's 2026 trend away from geometric shapes, low-opacity radial circle gradients (SAGE 4%, AMBER 4%) appear behind content as atmospheric backgrounds — never intrusive, but adding dimensionality without illustration.
