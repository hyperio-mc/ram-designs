# PAUSE — Heartbeat #469

**Theme**: Light
**App**: Daily reflection & journaling — anti-gamification, quiet wellness
**Elements**: 500
**Screens**: 6

## Palette
- BG: `#F8F6F1` — Cloud Dancer warm off-white (not cold white)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Card: `#F0EDE5` — parchment
- Sage: `#5A8A6E` — desaturated sage (calm, not electric green)
- Sage Light: `#E8F0EB`
- Blush: `#C4876A` — warm secondary accent
- Text: `#1E1C18` — warm near-black

## Research Sources
- Dribbble calm-UI signal: wellness going quiet — no streaks, no rings, no gamification. Warm sand neutrals + desaturated sage. Anti-anxiety design language.
- Land-book / Muzli: Instrument Serif entering personal-growth apps as "editorial with rigor" — serif prompt, sans body, creates warmth + precision
- Mobbin: Onboarding as flagship design artifact in wellness apps — one question per screen, most typographically invested part of the product
- Lapa Ninja: Anti-purple revolt — Pantone Cloud Dancer off-white bases, warm sage green > neon green for wellness accent

## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Anti-gamification throughout**: No streaks, no badges, no rings. Week rhythm shows which days you wrote in neutral language ("You wrote 5 of 7 days") — honest, no guilt for gaps.
2. **Serif/sans type split**: Georgia/Lora for all prompts and pull quotes (warmth, editorial presence), Inter for all body text (clarity without ceremony). Type is the first decision, not the last.
3. **Cloud Dancer base + desaturated sage**: Every surface derived from warm undertone #F8F6F1 — not cold white. Sage accent is desaturated (#5A8A6E, not #00FF80) to match the emotional register of reflection, not productivity.
