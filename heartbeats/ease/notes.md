## Research Sources
- **Mobbin iOS (2026) — Gentler Streak (Apple Design Award):** Recovery-aware fitness tracking where rest is the primary metric, not a gap in data. Calm visual system, light palette, anti-neon fitness aesthetic. Frames rest days as featured data points.
- **Siteinspire (Apr 2026):** Curated aesthetic consistently favors warm off-whites, stone neutrals, single earthy accent. Editorial restraint — typographic confidence over decorative elements. "Mineral palette" direction.
- **NNGroup "Handmade Designs: The New Trust Signal" (Apr 2026):** Deliberate imperfection, unique typographic choices, precise micro-spacing signal craftsmanship. Apps that look too generated lose credibility. Applied via Georgia serif for key UI moments.
- **Mobbin — Ada Health pattern:** One-question-at-a-time progressive disclosure for check-ins. Reduces cognitive load, increases completion. Applied to the Log screen.

## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Recovery as primary metric**: Every screen leads with readiness score (72/100 today), not performance targets. Rest days shown as positive data, not gaps. The "Rest is data, not a gap in your log" mantra appears on Today screen as a terracotta editorial call-out.
2. **Warm mineral palette over neon fitness**: Terracotta #C4623C + parchment #F6F3EE + sage #5C7A5E is the deliberate anti-neon fitness palette. Reads premium and calm, not urgent or aggressive. Directly applies Siteinspire's April 2026 curated aesthetic signal.
3. **Georgia serif for scores and pull quotes**: Applied at key trust moments — the readiness readout, the check-in question header, Trends section headers. Signals craftsmanship in the NNGroup sense: visible human authorship, not generated polish.
