## 3 Key Decisions
1. **Electric Coral not Purple**: AI video tools often use purple/blue. Coral cuts through as cinematic heat — visceral, alive, more film-like than tech-like.
2. **Three-track timeline**: VIDEO (violet/coral/teal clips) + AUDIO (amber waveform bars) + AI (coral-outlined AI enhancement spans) — each track has its own color language so the editor reads instantly.
3. **vidThumb() helper**: Video thumbnail simulation uses layered rects (bg hue + 4% sheen) + play circle + ratio pill + duration badge. Makes 6 library items feel like a real video gallery without actual images.
